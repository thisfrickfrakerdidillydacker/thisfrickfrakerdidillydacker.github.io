In The Name of The CommonWealth



In the CommonWealth's name
You bind their sacred wings
Making us the ones to blame

You say itś justice but just the same
The pain to them forever clings
In the people´s name

As you play your sacred game
Caring not the pain you bring
Making us the ones to blame

The innocent and guilty you maime
For justice? Your queen and king?
In the CommonWealths name

You cage them like animals to tame
You keep them as if they were things
Making us the one to blame

You let them speak but just the same
You blind our ears, don't hear them sing!
In the CommonWealths name
Making us the ones to blame



Authors note:



When someone is tried for a crime in this country they are tried "In the name of the Commenweath". The CommenWealth in this instsance refers to the state, as well as the people that reside within, meaning that any unjust ruleing is, as far as they are concered all of our faults as well as those more directly involved. That is symbolicly sensible as by suporting the system and alowing it to continue we are passively condoneing the justice systems' crimes.
