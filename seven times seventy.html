Seven times seventy:

You had caught him once, the law broken in his hands
So You said to yourself
To them
To us
He is guilty
We are just

we will cage him for a time
To learn how to be free

But you did not teach him how to be free
And so he returned to you once more
In chains, holding broken the law that you adore

you told him “you disappoint me son”
“But the mercy of justice is not yet done”
we’ll cage you once more and once more you'll be freed
Some time behind bars is just what you need”

But no place was made for him outside of those bars
So he returned, law broken in his arms
And so you told him
“You’ve broken our laws,
So sacred and dear
We’ll lock you away
Forever I fear
You can't be forgiven
that much is quite clear”

So you cast him away
and forgot to which place
And You said to yourself
To them
To us
They were guilty
We are just

But this I say to you:
What of the lord
Could you not find it to forgive them once more?

“Once more and once more oh where does it end?”
At seven then seventy times again
Then another, and some more

So remember forgiveness
you are drowning in gore
